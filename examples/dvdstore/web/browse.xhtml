This is the DVD search screen. Use the search box to the right to search for DVDs. The search component is a conversational Seam component. You can perform multiple searches in multiple windows, and Seam will keep them all separate. The shopping cart, of course, is shared, allowing the customer to place items in the cart from any of the search screens.

The pageflow is defined by a jBPM pageflow definition.
